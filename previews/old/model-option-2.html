Section: Methodology

Methodology

The Scout uses large language models to analyze each matchup the way a coaching staff would break down film. It evaluates tempo, defensive efficiency, key player matchups, coaching tendencies, and situational factors like tournament experience. Each game gets a structured analysis prompt, and the model picks the team with the stronger overall matchup profile.

Unlike pure statistical models, The Scout incorporates qualitative factors that are difficult to quantify: locker room chemistry signals, coaching track records in high-pressure games, and how a team's playing style historically performs in the tournament format. This mirrors how the best human analysts make predictions -- blending data with contextual understanding.
