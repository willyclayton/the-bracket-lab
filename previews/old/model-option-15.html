Methodology

The Scout processes structured team profiles, recent game logs, and coaching tendencies through a large language model, generating narrative matchup analysis for every possible first-round pairing before the bracket is announced.

Where The Scout excels is in identifying teams that outperform their seed due to intangibles: a hot shooting guard, a smothering zone defense, or a head coach with a track record against high seeds — signals that raw statistics often miss.

It weights recent form and coaching adjustability before finalizing picks, giving it an edge in identifying tournament-ready teams that are peaking at the right time.
