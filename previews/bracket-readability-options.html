Bracket Readability Options

Problem: When a model picks Missouri in R64 and Missouri loses, the R32 pick still shows Missouri without any visual signal that this pick is already dead.
Also: Clicking a matchup shows the pick but not whether it was correct or wrong.

Below are 5 different visual treatments. Each shows: (1) the R64 wrong pick, (2) the R32 "busted cascade" pick, and (3) an updated popover with verdict.

Option 1
Ghost + Strikethrough
Busted cascade picks fade to 30% opacity with a strikethrough on the team name. The entire card becomes a ghost — you can still read it, but it's clearly dead. The popover gets a verdict banner at the top.
