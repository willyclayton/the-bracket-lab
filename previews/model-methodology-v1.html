Process

01

Pull KenPom data

AdjOE, AdjDE, and Tempo for all 68 tournament teams.

02

Compute win probabilities

Logistic regression (0.18 coefficient) converts efficiency margin differential into win probability.

03

Run 10,000 simulations

Full 63-game bracket simulated independently. Win probabilities fixed across all sims.

04

Aggregate most-frequent outcomes

For each game slot, the team that advanced most often is selected.

05

Lock March 19

ESPN Tournament Challenge bracket filled out as timestamp proof.

Under the Hood

Key Inputs

  • AdjOE (adjusted offensive efficiency)
  • AdjDE (adjusted defensive efficiency)
  • Tempo (possessions per 40 min)
  • Efficiency margin (AdjOE - AdjDE)

Data Sources

  • KenPom (AdjOE, AdjDE, Tempo)
  • 15-year calibration dataset (2010-2025)

Assumptions

  • Efficiency margin is the single best predictor
  • 0.18 logit coefficient is stable across eras
  • Season averages represent tournament quality

Limitations

  • No style-of-play mismatch modeling
  • Completely blind to injuries
  • Conference tournament noise in late ratings
