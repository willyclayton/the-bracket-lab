Layout Option 4: Meet the Models

Thesis: First-time visitors don't know what they're looking at. Walk them through as a story. Educational without a text wall.

Best for: Pre-tournament (March 10–19). Brand new visitors. Shareable / viral potential.

Tradeoffs:
+ Narrative scroll creates genuine engagement and personality investment
+ Each model gets breathing room — personality leads, not just a card
+ Voting payoff at the end feels earned (users have context before choosing)
+ Scroll-triggered fade-in creates momentum and anticipation
+ Sticky dot-nav lets users jump without getting lost
− Long scroll — users may not reach the vote widget (mitigation: Z-pattern hero with "Meet them" CTA)
− Works best pre-tournament; during tournament, scores/accuracy are missing from sections
− SEO/above-fold: the real content is below the fold by design — fine for this use case
