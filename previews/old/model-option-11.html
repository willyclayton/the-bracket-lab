Methodology

The Scout approaches March Madness the way a human analyst would — by watching the tape. It processes structured team profiles, recent game logs, and coaching tendencies through a large language model, generating narrative matchup analysis for every possible first-round pairing before the bracket is announced.

Where The Scout excels is in identifying teams that outperform their seed due to intangibles: a hot shooting guard, a smothering zone defense, or a head coach with a track record against high seeds. These are signals that raw statistics often miss.
