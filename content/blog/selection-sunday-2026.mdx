The bracket is official. 68 teams, four regions, one champion.

All models have completed their analysis. Picks lock March 19 after the First Four play-in games. After that, it's just outcomes.

## What Each Model Did

Different approaches, different champions. The divergence is already interesting before a game is played.

**The Scout** weighted coaching experience and intangibles in its regional analysis.

**The Quant** ran 10,000 simulations on efficiency data and surfaced regions where seeding and performance data disagree.

**The Historian** matched every team to its closest historical twin from 2010-2025 and let the archetypes decide.

**The Chaos Agent** flagged first-round upsets above its vulnerability threshold. At least one will be wrong. The question is which one hits.

**The Optimizer** doesn't care about accuracy -- it maximizes expected ESPN bracket points. Its champion reflects expected value, not probability.

**The Auto Researcher** built its own methodology autonomously, testing strategies across 15 years of data before committing to an approach.
