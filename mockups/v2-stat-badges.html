Stat Badges on Collapsed Rows

Three approaches to surfacing key stats without expanding a pick. Shown on Lock, Upset, and Trap picks.

Variant A — Pill Badges

Small colored pills below the pick line. Shows all relevant stats for that pick type.

✓ (1) Duke over (16) Norfolk St. 9/9 ▼
+14.6 EM 1.2% hist upset LOW risk
✓ (2) Alabama over (15) Robert Morris 9/9 ▼
+11.2 EM 6.7% hist upset LOW risk
⚡ (10) Arkansas over (7) Nevada 6/9 ▼

⚠ (4) Arizona over (13) Yale 5/9 ▼

Variant B — Inline Text

No pills. Just colored monospace text separated by dots. Lighter, more editorial feel.
