How It Works

The Scout feeds every matchup into a large language model with detailed team profiles — pace of play, defensive rating, key player matchups, recent schedule strength, and coaching track record in high-pressure games.

The LLM synthesizes this into film-room style analysis for each pairing, identifying which team holds the structural advantages. It then ranks teams by projected dominance within their region before making final picks.

Key differentiator: The Scout weights coaching adjustability and lineup rotations — factors invisible to statistical models but critical in short-series tournament basketball.
