Methodology

The Scout approaches tournament prediction through the lens of a human basketball analyst — it processes structured team data including pace-of-play, defensive efficiency, key player matchup profiles, and recent form into a large language model.

The LLM synthesizes this data into film-room quality narrative analysis for every possible first-round matchup, identifying structural advantages that raw statistics obscure: coaching adjustability, lineup rotations, mental toughness signals.

After initial analysis, The Scout applies a calibration pass that weights teams that have recently outperformed their preseason metrics — a proxy for tournament readiness — before finalizing picks.
