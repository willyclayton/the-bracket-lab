> bracketlab describe --model scout
The Scout uses large language models to analyze each
matchup the way a coaching staff would break down film.
It evaluates tempo, defensive efficiency, key player
matchups, coaching tendencies, and situational factors
like tournament experience.
Each game gets a structured analysis prompt, and the
model picks the team with the stronger overall matchup
profile.
