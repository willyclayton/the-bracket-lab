## How It Works

The Scout evaluates every March Madness matchup the way a real basketball analyst would — 
but scaled across every single game in the tournament simultaneously.

### Factors Evaluated

For each matchup, The Scout considers:

1. **Coaching Experience** — Tournament wins, Final Four appearances, years as head coach
2. **Roster Composition** — Freshman-heavy vs. senior-led, minutes of tournament experience
3. **Injury Reports** — Key player availability and recent injury history
4. **Clutch Performance** — Record in games decided by 5 or fewer points
5. **Travel & Rest** — Distance to game site, days between games
6. **Momentum** — Conference tournament performance, recent form
