March Madness 2026 · Tournament Edition

Five Minds, One Bracket

An experiment in artificial intelligence, sports prediction, and the beautiful chaos of March.

← EDITORIAL INTRO (MULTI-COLUMN)

Every March, millions of Americans fill out a bracket. Most rely on gut feeling, fandom, or whatever ESPN's talking heads said that morning. We decided to try something different: give five AI models the same data and the same assignment -- predict every game of the 2026 NCAA Tournament -- and see which approach actually works.

Each model uses a fundamentally different strategy. One thinks like a scout, studying film and matchups. Another runs 10,000 statistical simulations. One mines 20 years of tournament history for patterns. One actively hunts for upsets. And one was simply told: "Figure it out yourself."

All picks were locked into ESPN Tournament Challenge before the first game tipped off. No edits, no adjustments, no human intervention. Just five different theories about what wins in March, tested against reality in real time.

← MODEL PROFILES (EDITORIAL CARDS)

The Competitors

🎬
Model 01
The Scout
LLM Matchup Analyst
"Film room intelligence at machine speed."
The Scout approaches each matchup the way a basketball analyst watches film. It reads team strengths, coaching tendencies, pace of play, and stylistic mismatches to make game-by-game decisions.
📊
Model 02
The Quant
Monte Carlo Simulation
"10,000 simulations. Zero feelings."
Pure probability. 10,000 Monte Carlo simulations using offensive and defensive efficiency, tempo, and strength of schedule.
📜
Model 03
The Historian
Archetype Matching
"Every team has a twin. History already played this game."
Matches 2026 teams to their closest historical analogues, then plays out the bracket based on what happened when similar teams met in past tournaments.
🔥
Model 04
The Chaos Agent
Upset Detector
"Your bracket is too safe. This one isn't."
Engineered to find vulnerable favorites and pick upsets with conviction. When the data says a lower seed has a real shot, this model always takes the swing.
🤖
Model 05
The Agent
Autonomous AI Researcher
"No rules. No prompts. Just: build me a bracket."
An autonomous AI agent given total freedom. It chose its own data sources, built its own methodology, and filled out its bracket without any human guidance whatsoever.
