The Five Competitors

🎬
The Scout
LLM Matchup Analyst
"Film room intelligence at machine speed."
An LLM analyzes every matchup the way a scout would watch film -- reading strengths, weaknesses, stylistic edges, and coaching tendencies to pick winners game by game.
📊
The Quant
Monte Carlo Simulation
"10,000 simulations. Zero feelings."
Runs 10,000 Monte Carlo simulations using efficiency metrics, tempo, and strength of schedule. The bracket is whatever outcome probability says it should be.
📜
The Historian
Archetype Matching
"Every team has a twin. History already played this game."
Matches every 2026 team to its closest historical analogue from past tournaments, then plays out the bracket based on what those historical twins actually did.
🔥
The Chaos Agent
Upset Detector
"Your bracket is too safe. This one isn't."
Specifically engineered to identify vulnerable favorites and likely upsets. If the data says a 12-seed has a real shot, this model takes it every time.
🤖
The Agent
Autonomous AI Researcher
"No rules. No prompts. Just: build me a bracket."
An autonomous AI agent given one instruction: "Research and build the best March Madness bracket you can." It decided its own methodology, data sources, and strategy.

How It Works

Models build brackets

Each model uses a completely different approach to fill out a full 63-game bracket before the tournament starts.
